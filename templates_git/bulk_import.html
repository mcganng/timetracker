How to Use
  1. LOG INTO SAVANT:
  2. Click on your TE Section
  3. On a row with the status of Historical click on the Pencil to the right
  4. Under the Type column click on the first cell in the row this could be "Job" or "Absence" and a blue highlight box will show
  5. Once that cell is highlighted press 'Ctrl' and 'A' to hightlight all cells
  6. Once all cells are highlighted press 'Crtl' and 'C' to copy
  1. LOG INTO TIME TRACKER:
      2. A) If there is a new project:

  2. If existing projects or finished adding new projects:
  3. Click on the Bulk Import Tab
  4. Set your Monday Date according to the date in Savant
  5. Paste your information from Savant in the lower box
  6. Click Process Import
